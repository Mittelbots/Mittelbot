## Moderation
----

    - unban [userid] [reason]
    - infraction [id]
    - infractions [mention/userid]
    - kick [mention/userid] [reason]
    - mute [mention/userid] [time] [reason]
    - unmute [mention/userid] [reason]
    - warn [mention/userid] [reason]

    - isbanned [mention/userid]
